From experience running large projects, I've developed some personal frameworks that have helped me ship better work.

## Start with the outcome

Before anything else, be clear about what done looks like. A fuzzy goal produces fuzzy results.

## Work backwards

Once you know the outcome, reverse-engineer the milestones. What must be true one week before launch? One month before?

## The weekly review

Every week, ask: what did I ship, what's blocked, and what do I need to decide?
